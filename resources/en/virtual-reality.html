What is Virtual Reality?

Virtual reality (VR) is a simulation of reality, in which an environment is imitated via a computer for a realistic experience for various senses. This can be done using HMD (a kind of glasses that you wear on your head) and headphones that provide the sound. Sometimes with special gloves that imitate touch and sometimes even with scent. VR is widely used in entertainment and gaming, but also has applications in education, training, healthcare, therapy, architecture and communication.
