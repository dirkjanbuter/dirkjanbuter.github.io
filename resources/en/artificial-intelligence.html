What is artificial intelligence?

Artificial intelligence (AI) means that machines, such as computers, are programmed to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. The idea is that these machines will be able to learn, reason and solve problems in a way that resembles human intelligence. For example, they can recognize speech, understand images, make decisions and even learn new things. The goal is to make machines smarter, so they can perform tasks that previously could only be done by humans.
