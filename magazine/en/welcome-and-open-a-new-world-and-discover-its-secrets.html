Who is Dirk Jan Buter?

Dirk Jan Buter is 44 years old and lives in Zwolle, Netherlands. He is married and has a seven-year-old daughter. He has been a freelance software developer for about eight years. He tried to start a radio station for handicapped people. That attempt was both a success and a failure. Success in the way it helped handicapped people, a failure because he could not hold it for long. He did that, with a lot of joy, for about a year.

He has signed up for cryonics, which means that he wants to freeze his body in liquid nitrogen after his death, in the hope that later, when the technology is ready, he can continue his life after his body is repaired.

He is writing a book about his life and experiences, his focus on the battle with illness, his journey to become an entrepreneur, and his future vision.

He regularly organizes meetups about technology for personal development and can be found in Amsterdam, Groningen, Zwolle, and Nijmegen.

In addition, he just started Dirk Jan Buter's Magazine, where he want to inspire tech enthusiasts and share knowledge in biohacking, artificial intelligence, and more. The Magazine consists of member stories, interviews with experts and innovators, tutorials, and reviews.
